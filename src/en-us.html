Lomadee

Lomadee is the leader in the affiliate program market. This is a platform where users can create an account to advertise products from different online stores. On any approved purchase made on the website, the affiliate will earn a commission from the advertiser. Lomadee is a website that links the affiliate to the advertiser.

Project Participation: I am currently the designer of this project in the areas of UI & UX and Developer. I designed and developed the new site layout to highlight and explain the difference between the affiliate and the advertiser. For the presentation design, I had to use the Hotjar tool to better understand the navigation and to be able to present some possible improvements. I am responsible for managing the administration, blog and social media related to the site. In large projects like this, I use my professional experience to provide a clean layout that gives users confidence.

Uello

Uello is a collaborative solution for urban shipping and delivery that delivers fast and offers highly customizable and affordable deliveries to small and medium-sized businesses. Uello synchronizes the supply chain with deliverers and consolidation points, enabling the development of well-sequenced routes that share the cost of execution between the different departments that compose them.

Project Participation: The main idea of this project was to present the functionalities of the company using animations, indicating to the user the available service of the company. By making an analogy between the relationships between people, I created an animation system that goes from one point to another by identifying the user's navigation on the site.

Modait

ModaIt began as a fashion blog aggregator to help women follow the trends of many bloggers at the same time. With the success of the brand, ModaIt has created a feature to help users buying the same clothes that the models wear on the photos. The content team looks for the most similar clothes in online stores, giving the user more opportunities to stay abreast of fashion trends. In 2016, the Modait market was launched on the VTEX e-commerce website.

Project Participation: With the success of the brand, Moda It has created a feature that helps users buying the same clothes as bloggers and models. At the beginning of 2013, concerns about smartphone browsing increased and it was around this time that I started using @media, used in CSS to adapt the layout to different devices. I also worked with the VTEX e-commerce platform, in which we implemented the functionality of keeping market information and inserting this information into the products offered by the site when browsing. This project has broadened my vision of the importance of fashion and its connection to people's lives. Involved in a project that speaks directly about fashion and beauty, I have been able to work with creative freedom in development and colour analysis.

SaveMe

SaveMe was founded in 2010 and it is the first site of collective shopping and coupon of reduction allowing the user to find offers more easily and more quickly. As a result, Saveme has become so important that it has extended its brand to other Latin American countries and has become Brazil's largest communication channel of discounts with over 300 real-time users and more 3,200,000 pages viewed during the month.

Project Participation: Saveme was the longest project in which I too part and that allowed me to benefit from a good HTML and CSS user interface experience. As part of a team of 12 people, the programmers and I, being the only designer, worked simultaneously with the Scrum teams of 6 people within 10-day sprints. I was responsible for the design and implementation of the pages according to the objectives of each sprint. In addition, I tested new management implementations that helped the content team publishing new offers. During the Black Friday, the most important day for the company, I was responsible for updating and formatting e-mails and creating banners with registered offers. During the six past years of Black Friday, I was able to gain experience working in major events that require a lot of commitment and attention. SaveMe has been a milestone in my professional career where, in addition to improving my knowledge, has helped me learning about other areas such as Google Analytics, Javascript, social networking and email marketing.
